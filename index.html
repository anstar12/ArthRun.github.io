Introducing ArthRun

Our mission is to prepare aspirants for the UPSC Preliminary and Mains and other competitive exams so that they can update themselves with the daily news analysis, facts, information in the field of economics. Founded in 2020, ArthRun has led numerous successful initiatives that have produced real change, in the competitive environment through different activities like weekly updates with a static portion of the topic, quizzes, flow charts, and mind mapping on different hot topics of the week.

Q1.Consider the following statements - 1. TRISHNA is a joint satellite mission between India and France 2. India jointly launched the International Solar Alliance with France 3. Both India and France are members of the G7 4. The Indian constitution ideals of ‘Liberty, Equality, Fraternity’ are borrowed from France. Which of the above statements is/are correct?

1,2 & 3 only 2,3 & 4 only 1,3 & 4 only 1,2 & 4 only

Q2.Consider the following statements regarding the Lokayukt- 1. Dr. L. M. Singhvi coined the term. 2. He is appointed by the Lokpal at the centre. 3. His powers differ state to state. 4. The tenure of his office is 5 years throughout the country. Which of the above statements is/are correct?

1 & 2 only 2 & 3 only 1 & 3 only None of the above

Q3. Consider the following statements regarding National Voters’ Day- 1. It is celebrated on January 25 2. National Awards for the Best Electoral Practices are conferred on the day Which of the above statements is/are correct?

1 only 2 only Both 1 & 2 None of the above
